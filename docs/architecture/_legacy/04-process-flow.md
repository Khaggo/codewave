# Process Flow

This document describes the operating flows that matter most to implementation.

## Lifecycle Event Types

### Administrative Events

These may be generated by the system:
- booking created
- booking confirmed
- booking rescheduled
- insurance inquiry submitted
- order placed
- invoice status updated

### Verified Condition Events

These should be backed by inspection records:
- intake condition confirmed
- pre-repair findings confirmed
- completion verified
- return issue diagnosed
- back job confirmed

## Main Booking and Service Flow

1. Customer registers and creates a vehicle profile.
2. Customer selects a service and time slot.
3. Booking is created and written to booking history.
4. Admin/staff reviews booking status if operational approval is needed.
5. On arrival, staff records an intake inspection.
6. Service is performed and job progress is monitored.
7. Staff records a completion inspection.
8. The vehicle timeline receives a verified completion milestone.
9. Loyalty and activity logs are updated.

## Insurance Flow

1. Customer submits an insurance quotation inquiry.
2. Optional documents are uploaded.
3. Inquiry moves through tracking states.
4. Customer and admin can view current inquiry status.
5. Relevant insurance activity is added to the vehicle timeline.

## Back-Job Flow

1. Customer returns after previous work.
2. Staff opens a back job linked to the original job or service record.
3. Staff records a return inspection.
4. The issue is classified:
   - related rework
   - warranty-like return
   - new concern
5. Back-job progress is tracked.
6. Resolution and closure are written to the job history and vehicle timeline.

## Invoice Commerce Flow

1. Customer browses products.
2. Customer adds items to cart.
3. Customer places an invoice-based order.
4. Inventory is reserved or adjusted.
5. Invoice payment status is tracked.
6. Order completion feeds loyalty and analytics.

## Reporting Flow

- Bookings, inspections, back jobs, and order events feed read models.
- BullMQ workers rebuild dashboard summaries as needed.
- RabbitMQ events transfer cross-domain business facts.

## Implementation Notes

- Keep lifecycle and process flow separate from UI assumptions.
- Treat inspections as operational verification, not optional comments.
- Do not merge back jobs into generic booking returns without explicit linkage to original work.
